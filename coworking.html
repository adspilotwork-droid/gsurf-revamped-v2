The Problem

What breaks with generic broadband.

Most ISPs sell the same residential-grade line to everyone and hope it works. For coworking spaces, it doesn't — here's what we hear from teams switching to GSurf.

One Wi-Fi across 40 companies is a disaster

Every laptop sees every printer, every Chromecast, every shared drive. A startup finance team on the same broadcast domain as a competing one. A compliance nightmare waiting to happen.

Peak-hour speed complaints

Coworking peaks 10am–1pm and 3pm–5pm. When broadband is contended, complaints come at exactly those hours — from the members you can least afford to lose.

Churn is higher with mediocre Wi-Fi

A member who can't take a clean Zoom call will leave. You won't see it in the exit survey, but it drives the number. Wi-Fi is your product, whether you accept it or not.

The Solution

Every plan tuned to how you actually work.

These aren't generic features — each one is a direct response to a pain point our customers keep raising. We're a local operator. We listen, and we ship.

Per-company VLAN isolation

Every member gets an isolated VLAN. Their printers, shared drives, internal traffic are invisible to every other tenant. Onboarding takes 20 minutes from our dashboard.

Whole-floor Wi-Fi 6 mesh

Ceiling-mounted Wi-Fi 6 access points, tuned for your floor plan, managed by GSurf. One SSID per company, roaming handoff as members walk between zones.

Member-facing bandwidth dashboard

A branded dashboard your members log into. Their own usage, their company's bandwidth, network health. Transparency that shifts complaints to confidence.

Off-hours install windows

All commissioning, upgrades, migrations happen during hours you specify — typically 10pm–6am or weekends. Zero member disruption.

Per-tenant bandwidth quotas

Optional per-company caps so one heavy member doesn't saturate the pipe. Cap enforcement at the switch, not by policy.

Quarterly business review

Every quarter we sit down with you and review uptime, incidents, member feedback and bandwidth trends. A partner, not just a vendor.

Common questions

Quick answers before you book.

The questions we hear most from prospects in this segment. If yours isn't here, our sales team answers on a ten-minute call.

Q1

Can you isolate each member company?

Yes — every member company gets an isolated VLAN. Their printers, shared drives and internal traffic are invisible to other tenants. Onboarding a new member takes about 20 minutes from our dashboard.

Q2

Do you provide member-facing dashboards?

Yes — a branded dashboard shows each company their own usage, bandwidth consumption and network health. Transparency shifts member complaints into confidence in your Wi-Fi.

Q3

Can installs happen outside business hours?

Yes. Coworking commissioning, upgrades and migrations are scheduled during hours you specify — typically 10pm–6am or weekends — to avoid member disruption.

Q4

How big a floor can you serve?

Our whole-floor Wi-Fi 6 mesh supports coworking floors from 2,000 to 30,000+ sq ft. Starting plan is 500 Mbps; high-density floors move to 1 Gbps Ultra.
