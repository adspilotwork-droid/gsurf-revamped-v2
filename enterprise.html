Common questions

Before you book the survey.

Most questions come up before the site visit. Here are the ones we hear most — if yours isn't here, our sales team is happy to answer on a ten-minute call.

Q1

How long does install take?

Typical commercial install in Whitefield is five to ten working days from site survey sign-off, assuming the building is within our fiber footprint. Complex multi-floor or multi-site installs take two to three weeks.

Q2

What's the contract term?

Monthly billing has no commitment — cancel with 30 days notice. Annual billing is 12-month term with 16% discount. Longer commitments (24 or 36 months) available with additional discount on request.

Q3

Are there install charges?

Standard install within 100m of the nearest fiber termination is free on annual plans and ₹2,500 on monthly plans. Extended runs, underground crossings and multi-floor are quoted after the site survey.

Q4

Can I upgrade mid-contract?

Yes — upgrades happen in our console, no new install. We pro-rate the plan difference on the next bill. Downgrades mid-contract are allowed on a best-effort basis depending on tier.

Q5

Is there a BGP or dual-IP option?

Yes — for customers who need BGP announcement of their own IP space, or dual-link active-active configurations, we offer both. This is sized individually; talk to enterprise sales for scoping.

Q6

Do you serve outside Whitefield?

Our footprint is Whitefield, Brookefield, ITPL, Hoodi, Kadugodi, Mahadevapura, KR Puram, CV Raman Nagar and Kaggadasapura. For addresses outside this ring, it's case-by-case depending on fiber path.
