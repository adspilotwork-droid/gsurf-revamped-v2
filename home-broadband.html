Home vs Enterprise

What's different for homes?

Home plans are priced and supported for households, not offices. Here's exactly what that means — and what it doesn't.

Same fiber backbone

You're on the same physical network as our enterprise customers. The difference is the plan tier, not the infrastructure. Your home gets proper fiber, not re-sold wireless.

Same 24/7 NOC

The engineers who answer your call at 1am are the same ones supporting a 200-bed hospital in Whitefield. You're not triaged to a lower-tier call centre.

No static IP

Dynamic IP only. If you're hosting a CCTV system with remote viewing, a home VPN, or a game server — you'll want the 100 Mbps enterprise plan instead for its static IP.

Best-effort SLA

No contractual uptime. Faults get fixed on best-effort basis, typically within 24 hours. For business-critical work-from-home, upgrade to the enterprise tier.
