About

We're a Whitefield fiber operator.

GSurf is the enterprise broadband brand of Gaxiom Networking Solutions Pvt Ltd, a fiber operator headquartered at No. 8 Whitely Layout. Our founding team has three decades of combined network-operator experience at national ISPs. We started GSurf because enterprise fiber in Whitefield deserved a local operator who shows up.

Our Philosophy

Dense network, local people.

The big national ISPs treat Whitefield as a row in a spreadsheet. We treat it as the whole map. That shapes how we invest, how we hire, and how we respond.

We live here

Every engineer on our field team lives within 10km of Whitely Layout. When a fault is reported at 11pm, the engineer who responds isn't driving from another city.

Our fiber belongs to us

We own the fiber plant in our footprint — we didn't resell someone else's capacity. That gives us direct control over routing, upgrades, and fault resolution.

We answer the phone

Sales, support, engineering, accounts — every number on our website reaches a real person. Often the same person for years. Continuity matters in a five-year commitment.

We stay focused

We don't sell mobile plans, DTH, OTT bundles, or white-labelled home security. We sell fiber broadband and we try to be excellent at it. Every metre of cable is aimed at making that one thing better.

We invest in the neighbourhood

Every new fiber run densifies the network for everyone else. Your neighbour's install lowers the cost for the next person on that street. Over six years it compounds into the coverage map you see.

We measure ourselves honestly

Our uptime is monitored at every customer's router WAN port, reported monthly, and shared openly on request. We don't report only good months. Transparency is how trust is built over years.

You don't build a great network by trying to serve every pincode in the city. You build it by serving one pincode excellently, then another, then another. That's the Whitefield plan.

— Founding team, GSurf · 2019
