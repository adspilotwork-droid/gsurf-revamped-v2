The Problem

What breaks with generic broadband.

Most ISPs sell the same residential-grade line to everyone and hope it works. For manufacturing units, it doesn't — here's what we hear from teams switching to GSurf.

Generic ISPs don't reach factories

The commercial broadband map stops at the last tech park. A manufacturing unit 5km further gets quoted six weeks for survey, eight weeks for install, and a "difficult access" premium.

OT and IT traffic must stay separate

Your PLCs, SCADA, ERP, CCTV, office Wi-Fi cannot share a flat network. A malware event on the office floor shouldn't ever reach a production line.

Monsoon and heat eat indoor gear

Consumer-grade ONTs die in a factory environment within a year. Dust, vibration, humidity, sustained 40°C — replacement becomes a quarterly expense.
