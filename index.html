Why GSurf

Infrastructure-first, locally accountable.

Every GSurf line is planned, laid and maintained by a team based in Whitefield itself. No outsourced support desks. No scripted call centers. When your connection flags, a named engineer from our Whitely Layout office is on your floor within four hours — or we credit the month back.

Dedicated fiber, not shared

Enterprise connections are uncontended — the bandwidth on the contract is the bandwidth you get, at 3am and at 3pm. No peak-hour slowdowns.

SLA with service credits

99.9% monthly uptime guaranteed, measured at your router's WAN port. If we miss it, the breach credits back automatically on your next invoice.

Static IP included

Every enterprise plan includes a static IPv4 address. VPN endpoints, firewalls, CCTV access, remote-desktop gateways — without surprise add-on charges each year.

A real engineer answers at 2am

Our NOC in Whitefield is staffed around the clock by engineers we employ, not contractors. Tickets acknowledged within 90 seconds, triaged before your first coffee.

Scaling without rewiring

Upgrade from 100 Mbps to 1 Gbps without changing the line, without an engineer visit, without a new ONT. Provisioning is console-driven — before lunch.

Named account manager

Every enterprise customer is assigned a single point of contact who knows your network, team and billing cycle. One email, one phone number — for the full contract.
